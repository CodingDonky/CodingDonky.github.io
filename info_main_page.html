Brief Overview:

I've always wanted to make my own homebrew RPG. Ever since I fell in love with d&d and pathfinder, and then found myself lost in the overly complex combat systems that drag for hours and the limiting "character class" system that seems to railroad players into a handful of playstyles.

I suppose the point of this system is to be what I wanted d&d to be. A totally open ended adventure with constant dramatic tension. PCs have 3 health bars to manage, giving a constant feeling of pressure. Combat is fast and extremely deadly. There is a huge build diversity akin to Skyrim's or Dark Souls' classless systems, every point in every attribute matters. Synergies can be explored beyond "keep major attributes high".
