Spatiomancy Spell Pool_Cost AP Description
Mark Beacon 🌌 P 3 Permanently mark a creature, object, or location with an invisible beacon. These beacons are almost impossible to detect without the "Detect Anomaly" spell. Willing creatures and objects marked by the beacon can be summoned. The pool point cost is PERMANENT. You must dispel the beacon to regain.
Detect Anomaly 🌌 2 Sense direction of an anomaly such as: gravitational effects, portals, summoned creatures/items, and time dilation effects.
Pocket Portal 🌌 🌌 P 1 Create a small pocket dimension, at most 1m cubed. The "P" indicates that the pool cost is PERMANENT. As long as the pocket dimension exists, these pool points will not recover.
Reduce 🌌 🌌 2+ Reduce size of an object by up to 8x. Use on a creature requires a skill check. Every 2AP used ruduces size by 2x.
Blurry 🌌 x 3 3 Space becomes blurry in the immediate area around you akin to the uncertainty principle. Reduce difficulty of all Reaction checks by 2.
Enlarge 🌌 x 3 3 Increase the size of an object up to 4x. Every 3AP used increases size by 2x.
Summon Object 🌌 x 3 5 Summon a marked creature via wormhole.
Summon Creature 🌌 x 4 ++ 5 Summon a marked creature via wormhole. Larger creatures are harder to summon.
Teleport 🌌 x 4 2 Your body is warped to another point in space that you can clearly see. Teleportation over longer distances is innaccurate and potentially dangerous.
Teleswap 🌌 x 5 3 Two creatures of approximately the same size swap places instantaneously. They must both be clearly visible and nearby.
Timestop 🌌 x 8 1 Stop time for 6AP worth of your actions. Spells you cast in the frozen timespace take effect the moment time resume.
