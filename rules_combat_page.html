Conundrums:

Conundrum are all the puzzles and difficult decisions you encounter during play. You can expend mental on thinking really really hard about things, potentially at the detriment of your intelligence pool. Thinking too much can burn out your brain after all, you will lose consciousness if you expend your mind too much.

Some mental abilities, such as "insight" will reveal truths about the world if the user can pass certain checks.


Distance:

Many spells and abilities have restrictions in the distance they can reach. Distances are simplified to a handful of categories as presented below.

Longbows are most effective within medium range. Some bows will get damage bonuses if you are in nearby range or close range. Trying to aim beyond medium range will make the target much more difficult to hit. The difficulty of hitting a target increases by 1 for every range step beyond the nearby range.

Symbol Distance Cap
T Touch
C Close. (< 5m)
N Nearby. (< 20m)
M Medium. (< 50m)
F Far. (< 100m)
E Eyesight. (< 1000m)
