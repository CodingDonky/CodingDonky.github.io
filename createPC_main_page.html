Creating your Character:

1 - Decide on the general vibe of your character.

2 - All of your Attributes start at 1. Distribute 13 initial Attribute points amongst your attributes. Your highest Attribute may not be more than 2 more than your second highest.

3 - Roll 1d6 and subtract 1, the result is your number of LUCK POINTS, or LP. Keep track of your LP, you will spend it for advantages in life during steps four and five below. You may ignore all LP rules if it suits your character and the DM allows it.

4 - Pick your birth circumstance. Spend LP to choose.

5 - Choose your childhood. Spend LP to choose.

6 - All remaining LP becomes BP. Choose your starting career.

7 - Buy starting land, tools, and equipment using your BP. LINK

8 - Pick your name, a short term goal, and a long term goal.

Gaining a Level:

1 - Gain 2 attribute points. Highest attribute can never be more than 4 points above second highest attribute.

2 - Gain 3 skill points. Must put into at least 2 seperate skills.
