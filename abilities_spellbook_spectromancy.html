Spectromancy:

Spectromancy is all ghosts and souls baby. Spectre's are minds and souls that no longer possess bodies. Through this sacred art, spectres can be interacted with, utilized, and repurposed.


Spellbooks:
Spectromancy Spell Pool_Cost AP Description
Spectral Eye 🔕 2 A floating eye is formed, the caster has full 20/20 vision out of this eye. The eye can levitate and move around slowly, but cannot go more than 5m from the caster.
Spectre 🔕 🔕 3 Call over a wandering spectre to reveal itself. Many spectres will mindlessly aid the caster. Spectres of powerful entities retain much of their previous personality.
Scry 🔕 x 3 3 Your mind leaves your body and is transported to a location you have previously been. For ten seconds your spectre can observe this area.
Possession 🔕 x 4 3 Your spectre leaves your body and attempts to temporarily possess another creature. Can also be used to temporarily inhabit an object.
Seal 🔕 x 5 6 Forcefully seal a consciousness inside of an object. The subject is unharmed and can still think, but not much else. Destruction of the object releases the subject as a spectre. Powerful sealed individuals can still influence the world around them in small ways, typically by influencing minds and souls of lesser creatures.
Revanant 🔕 x 8 6 Functions as the spell "Seal", but is executed with more prowess. The sealed subject gains full animated control over its new "body". Folktales speak of animated skeletons, swords that fight on their own accord, intelligent mirrors, and talking animals possessing human intellect created by this spell.
