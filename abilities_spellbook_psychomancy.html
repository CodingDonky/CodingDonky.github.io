Psychomancy:

Most Psychomancy spells are much less effective on intelligent individuals, individuals in combat or similar adrenaline-filled situations, and can be broken by strong bursts of emotion or outside influences such as pain. All of the below spells affect ONE individual's mind unless otherwise stated.

Spells that influence the minds of others can only be successfully used on animals and beasts with the trainable "Animal Handling" skill.


Spellbooks:
Psychomancy Spell Pool_Cost AP Description
Message 🧠 1 Send a telepathic message to a nearby entity.
Presdigitate 🧠 🧠 2 Craft a minor illusion in the mind of a nearby entity for several seconds, only affects one sensory input. No fine detail possible. Examples: fabricate smell of a rotten egg, illusory stain, a single blank coin, a popping noise, fake heat, etc..
Read Intention 🧠 🧠 2 Recieve an involuntary mental "yes" or "no" from a subject's mind after asking a question. Can use to detect deception, guess motives, or simply interrogate.
Confusion 🧠 🧠 3 Subject gains the inebriated status for up to several minutes or until they snap themselves out of it. Possible temporary memory loss.
Charm / Enrage / Bore 🧠 x 3 3 Influence subject's mind to push them towards one of three states. Alters an entity's demeanor relative to their usual personality.
Suppress Presence 🧠 x 3 2 The target is unlikely to give you a second glance. Your presence is subdued to that of a ninja. Works best in crowded or dark areas. Alert targets may not be affected.
Mental Wall 🧠 x 3 P 5 Surround your mind with a mental wall, preventing influence all but the strongest mental spells. Alternatively, mental walls can be placed on entities on touch to suppress a memory, block the use of a school of magic, or nullify certain brain functions.
Sleep 🧠 x 4 2 Induce sleep. Requires touching subject's temple.
Illusory Object 🧠 x 4 6 Fabricate an illusory object, fashioned after something you know intimately. Can influence up to 2 senses, or more intracately affect 1.
Illusory Wall / Terrain 🧠 x 5 1 As "Illusory Object", but for a very large, simple, textured wall or similar terrain such as a tree, boulder, small hovel, or door.
Falsify Memory 🧠 x 5 6 Requires sustained touch on subject's temple. Overwrite a memory with a simple, falsified memory. Skill check required to fine tune the specifics of the memory.
Dominate 🧠 x 6 3 Dominate the subject's mental faculties. The subject is compelled to view you as their god, or equivalent. Low intelligence individuals are affected longest, up to several hours.
Shatter Psyche 🧠 x 8 3 A forbidden spell. Shatter an entity's mind. The user can decide on the specifics and the magnitude. Effects can include a semipermanent coma, insanity, permanent servitude, personality alteration, or similar.
